I identified several areas which I could improve:

  • The site was made with Bootstrap 3
  • It didn't pass the lighthouse tests
  • It scored poorly on security tests
  • It didn't comply with the GDPR policy

I built a new website from scratch. I still used their brand colors and most of the text they had. For design, I used the new version of Bootstrap 5.
I optimized all of their videos and images, serving .webp images.
No cookies are used, but due to a contact form a privacy policy is required. I created one with iubenda.com who offers services as privacy-policies, terms of use, cookies etc. and I also added the privacy and GDPR policy that Netlify provides.
I increased the security of the site, setting some headers, such as referer-policy, content-security-policy, x-frame options etc. That was a challenge as well but there's good documentation at mozilla.org.
The site is hosted in Netlify.

Explore_Suomi

Explore_Suomi is a blog website supplementary to the instagram account @explore_suomi which contains stories and photos of trips in Finland.

Tools I used

Read more

I built a new website from scratch. I chose to work with the PaperCss framework because it is very minimal, customizable with my own css, it looks funny and fresh.
As the blog grows there will be plenty of images, therefore I use an image-CDN to automatically optimize, transform the images in real-time, and storage them as well.
I use handlebars.js as a templating language. All the information of the posts (destination, description, images) are written in a javascript object and then injected by handlebars in html.
Finally the site is bundled with Snowpack and hosted in Netlify.
