Vakka-Viestintä

Vakka-Viestintä is an advertising agency located in Uusikaupunki (FI), specializing in digital advertisement and marketing. I was tasked to update and refresh their website.

Tools I used

Read more

I identified several areas which I could improve:

  • The site was made with Bootstrap 3
  • It didn't pass the lighthouse tests
  • It scored poorly on security tests
  • It didn't comply with the GDPR policy

I built a new website from scratch. I still used their brand colors and most of the text they had. For design, I used the new version of Bootstrap 5.1.
I optimized all of their videos and images, serving .webp images.
No cookies are used, but there is a contact form therefore a privacy policy is required. I had never before implemented a privacy policy and it was quite a challenge to find information. Luckily I stumped upon iubenda.com who offer services for privacy-policies, terms of use, cookies etc.
I increased the security of the site, setting some headers, such as referer-policy, content-security-policy, x-frame options etc. That was a challenge as well but there's good documentation at mozilla.org.
Finally the site is bundled with Snowpack and served in Netlify.
